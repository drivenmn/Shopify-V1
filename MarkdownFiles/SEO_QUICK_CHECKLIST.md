## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Reviews are King**: 5-10 new Google reviews per month = huge ranking boost
2. **Local Content**: Write about Minnesota-specific topics (winter car care, etc.)
3. **Photos Matter**: Upload new photos to Google Business Profile weekly
4. **Consistency**: NAP must be identical everywhere online
5. **Mobile First**: 70% of searches are mobile - optimize for it
6. **Speed Matters**: Aim for 2-3 second page load time
7. **Video Content**: Videos on your site increase time on page (good for SEO)
8. **Update Often**: Fresh content signals active business to Google
