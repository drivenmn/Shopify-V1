## Monthly SEO Audit Checklist

### Technical SEO
- [ ] Run Google PageSpeed Insights (target: 90+ mobile, 95+ desktop)
- [ ] Check all pages load in under 3 seconds
- [ ] Verify all images have alt text
- [ ] Check for broken links (use Screaming Frog or similar)
- [ ] Verify canonical tags on all pages
- [ ] Check robots.txt is accessible
- [ ] Verify sitemap.xml is up to date
- [ ] Test on mobile devices (iOS and Android)
- [ ] Check Core Web Vitals in Search Console

### On-Page SEO
- [ ] Review meta titles (under 60 characters)
- [ ] Review meta descriptions (under 155 characters)
- [ ] Check heading hierarchy (H1 ‚Üí H2 ‚Üí H3)
- [ ] Verify structured data with Rich Results Test
- [ ] Check for duplicate content
- [ ] Review keyword density (not too much)
- [ ] Update old content with fresh information

### Local SEO
- [ ] Verify NAP consistency across all platforms
- [ ] Check Google Business Profile optimization
- [ ] Monitor local pack rankings
- [ ] Check local citations (Yelp, Yellow Pages, etc.)
- [ ] Review and respond to all reviews
- [ ] Update business photos
- [ ] Post to Google Business Profile

### Content Audit
- [ ] Identify top-performing pages (double down on these)
- [ ] Identify low-performing pages (improve or remove)
- [ ] Check bounce rate on key pages (target: under 50%)
- [ ] Review time on page (target: 2+ minutes)
- [ ] Plan new content based on search queries

### Backlink Profile
- [ ] Check new backlinks (Ahrefs or Search Console)
- [ ] Identify and disavow spammy links
- [ ] Reach out for new link opportunities
- [ ] Monitor competitor backlinks

## Competitor Analysis

### Identify Top Competitors
Research who ranks for your target keywords:

1. **Direct Competitors** (same services, same area):
   - List competitors ranking in top 3
   - Analyze their website structure
   - Review their Google Business Profile
   - Check their review count/rating
   - Identify content gaps you can fill

2. **Keyword Opportunities**:
   - Find keywords competitors rank for but you don't
   - Target long-tail keywords with less competition
   - Create better content than competitors

### Monthly Competitor Checks:
- [ ] Check their Google Business Profile updates
- [ ] Review their new content
- [ ] Monitor their review count
- [ ] Check their service pricing
- [ ] Analyze their social media activity
